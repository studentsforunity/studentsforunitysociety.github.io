AFRICA

Region Leader: Anthony

The African Table expressed African cultures through it's uses of traditional African fabrics such as the Kente cloth, it's use of language immersion as a means of cultural exchange and the performance of the traditional Anansi stories.

They also played afro beats which often combines multiple cultural aspects of African diaspora from the beats often associated with African American culture to the instruments associated with African cultures.

Thank you TJ, Joyeuse, Thabit, Makeen, and Freddie for the entertaining play!
