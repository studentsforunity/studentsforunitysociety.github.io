To end the 2021-22 school year, we threw a bakesale to acknowledge the racial and ethnic diversity of BASIS Tucson North. Seven regions were represented: Africa, Philippines, China, India, Vietnam, Middle East, and Latin America. They all held beautiful stands with delicious foods! All profit made went towards the Annual Teacher Fund.
