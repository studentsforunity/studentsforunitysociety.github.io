BASIS Meet Yourself 2022

On November 18 of 2022, Students For Unity along with many volunteers held a cultural showcase for 9 regions: India, Philippines, Vietnam, China, Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Latin America. The regions threw a performance and held tables selling regional food and showing cultural traditions. Thank you to Athena for being the head organizer for the event!

Philippines

Region Leader: Blessy

The Philippines table sold delicious Filipino snacks, including turon, halo-halo, and various chips/crackers along with offering teacher cardboard cutouts, dried mangoes, and chocolate for the raffles. The Philippines table threw three performances: 1. Tinikling, a traditional folk dance involving two people dancing between two bamboo sticks being clicked. An amazing job to Kyla, Blessy, Mary Bless, William, Athena, and Therese! 2. Kahit Ayaw Mo Na, which translates to "Even if you don't want it anymore", is This Band's popular song encapsulates the emotions and hardships entailed in love. A beautiful cover done by Kiera! 3. Sarah Geronimo's hit song "Tala", translating to "star", is about the shining star within people. Thank you to Athena, Blessy, Kyla, and Kiera for ending the Filipino performances with a bang!
