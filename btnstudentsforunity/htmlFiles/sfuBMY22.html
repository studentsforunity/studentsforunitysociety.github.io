BASIS Meet Yourself 2022

On November 18 of 2022, Students For Unity along with many volunteers held a cultural showcase for 9 regions: India, Philippines, Vietnam, China, Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Latin America. Each region threw a performance and held tables selling regional food and showing cultural traditions. Thank you to Athena for being the head organizer for the event!
